Who They Are

Achille Pinto S.p.A. is an historic Italian textile company, established in 1933 and based in Como, Lombardy. The company is known for its high-quality textile production, with a strong presence in luxury fashion and interior fields. Its expertise spans silk, wool, and mixed fabrics.
[Source]

Activities & Product Range

  • Design and production of luxury textiles for fashion and interiors
  • Specialisations in silk, wool, linen, cotton, viscose and man-made fibres
  • Products: scarves, shawls, foulards, ties, fabrics for apparel and home interiors
  • Custom designs and tailor-made solutions for prominent brands
[Source]

Technological Innovation & Sustainability
